# FS PBX Product Context

## Register

product

## Users

FS PBX is used by PBX administrators and communications operators who configure tenants, extensions, routing, provisioning, FreeSWITCH, and supporting services. They work in an operational admin interface where clear status, predictable controls, and fast access to configuration matter more than presentation.

## Product Purpose

FS PBX provides a modern Laravel and Vue administration surface for managing a FreeSWITCH-based phone system. A successful interface makes complex telephony configuration understandable, keeps runtime and persisted state clear, and lets administrators act confidently without hiding important operational detail.

## Brand Personality

Practical, dependable, and direct. Copy is short and operational. The interface should feel consistent and familiar across administrative pages.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style admin UI, decorative dashboards, novelty interactions, inconsistent component patterns, and visual treatments that compete with operational data. Do not disguise technical state or destructive actions.

## Design Principles

- Reuse established FS PBX page patterns so administrators can transfer knowledge between screens.
- Make persisted configuration and live FreeSWITCH state easy to distinguish.
- Keep common actions visible, permission-aware, and explicit about their effects.
- Prefer compact, scannable information hierarchy over decorative presentation.
- Preserve useful detail while progressively grouping filters, categories, and actions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Preserve keyboard access, visible focus states, semantic controls, readable contrast, and text labels for important actions. Do not rely on color alone for status, and respect reduced-motion expectations by limiting animation to state feedback.
